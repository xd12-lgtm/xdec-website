# About XDEC

## Leading the Audio Industry for 20 Years

Shenzhen Xuanda Electronics Co., Ltd. (XDEC) is a professional speaker manufacturer with over 20 years of experience in the audio industry. We specialize in OEM/ODM services, providing high-quality audio solutions to customers worldwide.

### Our Story

Founded in 2004, XDEC has grown from a small startup to a leading professional speaker manufacturer in the audio industry. Over the past two decades, we have built a reputation for excellence, innovation, and reliability.

### Our Values

- **Quality First**: We maintain the highest standards in every aspect of our manufacturing process.
- **Innovation**: Continuous research and development to stay at the forefront of audio technology.
- **Customer Focus**: Your satisfaction is our priority. We work closely with you to meet your needs.
- **Reliability**: Trusted by hundreds of clients worldwide for consistent quality and service.
